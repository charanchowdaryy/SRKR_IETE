About SRKR IETE

SRKR IETE (Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers) is the professional student body forum of SRKR College's ECE department. It is dedicated to the professional development of students at SRKR College through the organization of various events and workshops.

Join us in our mission to shape the future of technology and create a better world through electronics and telecommunications!

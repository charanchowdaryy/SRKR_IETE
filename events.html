SRKR IETE EVENTS

ENCENDER-1:

Encender-1 stands as an engaging initiative tailored specifically for first-year students enrolled in the ECE department. It serves as a gateway to familiarize them with the department's ethos and the esteemed institution of IETE. Through this program, students gain invaluable insights into the facilities at their disposal, paving the way for enthusiastic participation in a diverse array of events organized by IETE.

ENCENDER-2:

Encender-2 is a purpose-driven interaction program meticulously curated for second-year students within the ECE department. Tailored to elevate awareness surrounding IETE, this initiative serves as a beacon, illuminating the manifold benefits of IETE membership and imparting invaluable career advice. Through this program, students are equipped with the knowledge and guidance essential for charting a path towards professional excellence within the realms of electronics and telecommunications.

PRE-QUEST:

Pre-Quest serves as a pivotal program orchestrated by executive body members to illuminate participants on presentation rules and topics. With a keen focus on unraveling students' innovative aptitude, its primary objective is to catalyze their exploration of emerging technologies and foster a culture of technological innovation and advancement. Through this initiative, participants are empowered to embrace new horizons and chart a course towards pioneering endeavors in their respective fields.

QUEST:

Quest stands as a dynamic presentation competition meticulously crafted to hone the presentation prowess of ECE students. It serves as a platform for participants to showcase their communication acumen and technical expertise, with winners being duly rewarded with prizes as a testament to their dedication and excellence. Through this event, participants are propelled towards professional growth and recognition, inspiring them to strive for continued excellence in their academic and professional endeavors.

PRE-CRUX:

Pre-Crux stands as an essential program facilitated by executive body members to provide participants with comprehensive guidance on project rules and innovative ideas. With a primary focus on fostering creativity and ingenuity, this initiative aims to inspire participants to present their projects with innovative approaches. Moreover, Pre-Crux serves as a platform to address any queries or uncertainties regarding projects, ensuring participants are well-equipped to embark on their project presentations with confidence and clarity.

CRUX:

Crux represents a pivotal hardware project presentation competition meticulously organized by SRKR IETE to elevate the technical acumen of ECE students while fostering a culture of practical knowledge and hands-on learning. Through this event, participants are provided with a unique opportunity to showcase their technical prowess, innovative thinking, and problem-solving abilities in the realm of hardware projects. By encouraging participation and recognizing excellence, Crux plays a pivotal role in nurturing the next generation of technologists and innovators within the field of electronics and telecommunications.
